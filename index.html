About Neuroway Network

Our Mission: Neuroway Network is dedicated to empowering neurodivergent individuals by providing tailored support, education, and community connections that foster growth and wellbeing.

About Me: I am the founder and lead practitioner of Neuroway Network, committed to integrating compassionate, evidence-based practices to support diverse neurodivergent needs.
